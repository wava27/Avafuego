What is good design?

To me, good design is simple, effective, and memorable. Lately, I have seen a lot of minimalistic designs on beauty and skincare products that evoke ideas of simplicity, sustainability, and healthy products. Nonsuprisingly, despite their fantastic claims, none have ever done anything but make my skin sticky and my wallet hurt. But, I still bought them--their design was compelling enough for me, as I'm sure it was for others. I am interested in minimalist designs, both in understanding why they are so effective and in trying my hand at creating some. Good design in consumables like this is about guiding perception and creating desire.
